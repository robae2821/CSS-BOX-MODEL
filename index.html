BLOOD DIAMOND

As civil war rages through 1990s Sierra Leone, two men,
a white South African mercenary (Leonardo DiCaprio) and
a black Mende fisherman (Djimon Hounsou), become joined
in a common quest to recover a rare gem that has the power
to transform their lives.With the help of an American journalist
(Jennifer Connelly), the men embark on ahazardous trek through
rebel territory to achieve their goal.
